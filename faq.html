Frequently Asked Questions

  • question_answer How does the product differ from competitors?
    Our product facilitates the transfer of information between a patient and a medical professional. With convenient and real-time tracking, the StressWatch can monitor the patient’s stress throughout the day. A medical professional could access the data through the database to keep track of progress of treatment or to identify stressors. This saves the patient time and money on regular consultations with a medical professional. It is also a cheaper and more reliable alternative to existing products.
  • question_answer Who can use the StressWatch?
    Those who see stress or anxiety as a problem in their lives may use the StressWatch. However, anyone can use it to take measurements, even if there are no known issues of stress or anxiety.
  • question_answer How can the StressWatch help me?
    The user can work with a health professional to evaluate their progress during treatment. In addition, the device can take measurements during specific events or in specific situations to identify stressors or triggers and measure the degree of stress or anxiety. The information can help the user to learn to cope with these triggers with a health professional.
  • question_answer How accurate is the StressWatch?
    The StressWatch is very accurate and is effective at detecting changes in stress levels. However, there are several factors that can influence results. The humidity of the patient’s environment can skew the skin conductance to lower resistance. Similarly, perspiration on a patient’s hands can lower the results.
  • question_answer How much does the device cost?
    The StressWatch costs $130, which is less than half the price of other competitors such as the $400 Apple Watch.
  • question_answer How do I use the StressWatch?
    Please refer to our demo video on the home page for a step-by-step guide.
  • question_answer How do I create an account?
    Simply click the "Sign Up" button at the top right of the website. Once you fill in all the required fields, a new page called "Your SweatWatch" will appear. Click on it to go to your StressWatch page to make your first measurement.
  • question_answer How can I view my data?
    Click on the "Login" button at the top right of the website and log in. Once you do, a new page called "Your SweatWatch" will appear. Click on it, scroll down, and you will be able to view your previous data in the form of a graph. If you want to view data for a specific day, you can do so by selecting a date at the bottom of the page.
